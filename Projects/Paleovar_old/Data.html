Data

Ocean sediments allow reconstructions of past conditions at the sea surface or in the deep sea.


Speleothems (cave deposits) such as stalagmites and stalagtites contain information on past local
environmental conditions such as precipitation, temperature and vegetation.



Ice cores: Bubbles in ancient ice retrieved from drilling
deep cores in the Greenland and Antarctic ice sheets contain
information on past atmospheric greenhouse gas concentrations.
