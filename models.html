Models

  • Model of Ocean Biogeochemistry and Isotopes (MOBI)

    Ocean Biogeochemistry: 2 nutrients (NO3, PO3), 2 phytoplankton, zooplankton, detritus (2N2PZD) model
    Isotopes: carbon (δ13C) and nitrogen (δ15N) isotopes

  • Oregon State University / University of Victoria Model (OSUVic)

    Global climate model
    Ocean: 3-dimensional General Circulation Model (GCM), 1.8x3.6 deg, 19 vertical levels
    Atmosphere: 3-dimensional GCM, T21/T42, 10 vertical layers
    Sea Ice: dynamic-thermodynamic
    Land: fixed vegetation model
    Ocean Biogeochemistry: MOBI

  • University of Victoria Earth System Climate Model (UVic ESCM)

    Global climate model of intermediate complexity
    Ocean: 3-dimensional General Circulation Model (GCM), 1.8x3.6 deg, 19 vertical levels
    Atmosphere: 2-dimensional Energy-Moisture Balance Model (EMBM), 1.8x2.6 deg, 1 vertical layer
    Sea Ice: dynamic-thermodynamic
    Land: dynamic vegetation model (TRIFFID/MOSES)
    Ocean Biogeochemistry: 2 nutrients (NO3, PO3), 2 phytoplankton, zooplankton, detritus (2N2PZD) model (MOBI)
